About the Conference

The Research Society for Information Technology & Artificial Intelligence (RSITAI) is proud to announce its upcoming international conference on Artificial Intelligence, set to take place in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania in March 2024. This prestigious event will gather leading researchers, academicians, stakeholders, and enthusiasts in the field of AI from around the globe. It aims to foster innovation, networking, and collaboration in the realm of AI, focusing on its advancements, applications, and challenges in Africa.


The conference will feature keynote speeches, engaging panels, hands-on workshops, and various high-value networking opportunities. We will delve into critical discussions on practical AI applications, data privacy, AI ethics, innovation, and more, all tailored to resonate with the technological strides and unique challenges faced on the African continent.


  1. Empowering African Industries with AI: Real-world applications and success stories.
  2. Data Privacy and Security in the Age of AI: Strategies for protecting sensitive information.
  3. Ethical AI: Developing frameworks for responsible AI adoption and use.
  4. Innovation and AI: Paving the way for a technologically advanced Africa.

Join us in unveiling the latest AI research, innovations, and practices that are set to redefine the technological landscape in Africa and beyond. Be part of a change-making journey as we explore how AI can contribute to sustainable development goals, industry growth, and societal betterment in African contexts.


Our Partners


Research IT AI is dedicated to evidence-based research and training, focusing on the advancement of intellectual property and information technology. Our goal is to understand and share knowledge on their development and influence on law and human rights within Tanzania and across Africa.
